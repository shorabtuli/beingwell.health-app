Rheumatoid arthritis is an autoimmune condition where your immune system mistakenly attacks the lining of your joints, causing inflammation, pain, and stiffness. Unlike osteoarthritis, which is wear-and-tear damage, RA affects both sides of your body and can impact other organs as well.
