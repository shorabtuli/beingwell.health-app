Arthritis App Ratings: Top Mobile Apps for RA Management

By Being Well Team January 15, 2024

Comprehensive ratings and reviews of the best mobile apps for arthritis management. Find the perfect app to help you track symptoms, manage medications, and improve your RA wellness.

How We Rate Arthritis Apps

Our ratings are based on user reviews, feature completeness, ease of use, and effectiveness for arthritis management. We evaluate apps across multiple criteria to help you find the best options for your needs.

Top-Rated Arthritis Apps

1. Being Well (4.8/5 Stars)

Our comprehensive RA management app offers personalized wellness plans, symptom tracking, and community support. Features include medication reminders, exercise routines, and progress monitoring.

2. MyRA (4.6/5 Stars)

Developed by the Arthritis Foundation, MyRA provides symptom tracking, medication management, and educational resources. The app is specifically designed for rheumatoid arthritis patients.

3. Arthritis Power (4.4/5 Stars)

A research-focused app that allows users to track symptoms and contribute to arthritis research. Features include pain tracking, medication logging, and research participation opportunities.

Key Features to Look For

  • Symptom Tracking: Daily pain, stiffness, and fatigue monitoring
  • Medication Management: Reminders and side effect tracking
  • Exercise Routines: RA-friendly workout programs
  • Community Support: Connection with other arthritis patients
  • Data Export: Ability to share information with healthcare providers

User Experience Considerations

When choosing an arthritis app, consider factors like ease of use, accessibility features, and whether the app accommodates users with limited mobility or dexterity. Look for apps with large buttons, clear navigation, and voice input options.

Privacy and Security

Ensure any app you choose has strong privacy policies and secure data handling practices. Your health information is sensitive, so verify that the app complies with relevant regulations and protects your data appropriately.

Cost Considerations

Many arthritis apps offer free versions with basic features, while premium versions provide additional functionality. Consider starting with free versions to test usability before committing to paid subscriptions.
