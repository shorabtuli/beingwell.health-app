Being Well vs Apple Health: Arthritis Management App Comparison

By Being Well Team January 15, 2024

Comprehensive comparison of Being Well and Apple Health for arthritis management. Discover which app better supports RA patients with specialized features and health tracking.

App Overview and Purpose

Both Being Well and Apple Health aim to help users manage their health, but they approach this goal differently. Understanding these differences can help you choose the right app for your arthritis management needs.

Being Well: Specialized Arthritis Management

Core Philosophy

Being Well is specifically designed for rheumatoid arthritis patients, offering comprehensive management tools, personalized wellness plans, and community support tailored to RA needs.

Key Features

  • RA-Specific Tracking: Symptom monitoring designed for arthritis patients
  • Personalized Wellness Plans: AI-generated plans based on RA assessment
  • Community Support: Connection with other RA patients
  • Medication Management: RA-specific medication tracking and reminders
  • Accessible Design: Interface designed for users with limited mobility

Apple Health: General Health Platform

Core Philosophy

Apple Health is a comprehensive health platform that aggregates data from various sources and provides general health insights for all users, not specifically arthritis patients.

Key Features

  • Data Aggregation: Collects health data from multiple apps and devices
  • General Health Metrics: Activity, heart rate, sleep, nutrition tracking
  • Device Integration: Works with Apple Watch and other Apple devices
  • Third-Party App Support: Connects with various health and fitness apps
  • Health Records: Integration with healthcare providers

Direct Feature Comparison

Health Tracking

Being Well: Specialized arthritis symptom tracking, pain monitoring, medication effects
Apple Health: General health metrics, activity tracking, vital signs monitoring

Personalization

Being Well: RA-specific recommendations, personalized wellness plans, community insights
Apple Health: General health insights, activity goals, basic health recommendations

User Experience

Being Well: Designed for arthritis patients with accessible interface and RA-focused features
Apple Health: Standard iOS interface, requires additional apps for specialized tracking

Integration and Data Sharing

Being Well

Focuses on internal data management and community sharing. Data is used to improve personalized recommendations and community support.

Apple Health

Free with iOS devices, but requires additional apps for specialized features. May have costs associated with connected apps and devices.

Which App is Right for You?

Choose Being Well If:

  • You have rheumatoid arthritis and want specialized management
  • You need RA-specific symptom tracking and recommendations
  • Community support from other arthritis patients is important
  • You prefer an accessible interface designed for limited mobility
  • You want comprehensive arthritis management in one app

Choose Apple Health If:

  • You want general health tracking across multiple areas
  • You use Apple devices and want ecosystem integration
  • You prefer to use multiple specialized apps
  • You need basic health monitoring and data aggregation
  • You want integration with healthcare providers

Using Both Apps Together

Some users may benefit from using both apps:

  • Use Being Well for specialized arthritis management
  • Use Apple Health for general health tracking and device integration
  • Sync relevant data between apps when possible
  • Use Apple Health as a central hub for overall health data

Conclusion

Both Being Well and Apple Health offer valuable health management tools, but they serve different needs. Being Well provides specialized arthritis management with RA-specific features, while Apple Health offers general health tracking and ecosystem integration. Choose the app that best aligns with your specific health management goals and preferences.
