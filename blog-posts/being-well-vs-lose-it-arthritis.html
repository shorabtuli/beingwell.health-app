Being Well vs Lose It: Arthritis Management App Comparison

By Being Well Team January 15, 2024

Comprehensive comparison of Being Well and Lose It apps for arthritis management. Discover which app better supports RA patients with nutrition, symptom tracking, and wellness planning.

App Overview

Both Being Well and Lose It offer valuable tools for health management, but they serve different primary purposes. Understanding these differences can help you choose the right app for your arthritis management needs.

Being Well: Specialized Arthritis Management

Key Features

  • RA-Specific Focus: Designed specifically for rheumatoid arthritis patients
  • Comprehensive Wellness: Covers all 8 wellness pillars including , hydration, and mental health
  • Personalized Plans: AI-generated wellness plans based on your specific RA symptoms and needs
  • Symptom Tracking: Detailed pain, stiffness, and fatigue monitoring
  • Community Support: Connection with other RA patients

Strengths for Arthritis Patients

Being Well excels in providing comprehensive arthritis management with personalized recommendations, symptom tracking, and community support specifically designed for RA patients.

Lose It: General Nutrition and Weight Management

Key Features

  • Calorie Tracking: Comprehensive food logging and nutritional analysis
  • Weight Management: Goal setting and progress tracking
  • Food Database: Extensive database of foods and nutritional information
  • Social Features: Community support for weight loss goals
  • Integration: Works with fitness trackers and other health apps

Strengths for General Health

Lose It is excellent for general nutrition tracking and weight management, with robust food logging capabilities and social support features.

Direct Comparison

Arthritis-Specific Features

Being Well: Comprehensive RA management with symptom tracking, medication reminders, and personalized wellness plans.
Lose It: Limited arthritis-specific features, primarily focused on nutrition and weight management.

Nutrition Tracking

Being Well: Basic nutrition tracking with anti-inflammatory diet focus.
Lose It: Advanced nutrition tracking with extensive food database and detailed nutritional analysis.

User Experience

Being Well: Designed for users with limited mobility and dexterity, with large buttons and accessible interface.
Lose It: Standard mobile app interface, may not accommodate users with severe arthritis symptoms.

Which App is Right for You?

Choose Being Well If:

  • You have rheumatoid arthritis and want comprehensive management
  • You need symptom tracking and medication reminders
  • You want personalized wellness plans
  • You value community support from other RA patients
  • You need an accessible interface for limited mobility

Choose Lose It If:

  • Your primary goal is weight management
  • You need detailed nutrition tracking
  • You want extensive food database and logging features
  • You're comfortable with standard mobile app interfaces

Conclusion

While both apps offer valuable health management tools, Being Well is specifically designed for arthritis patients and provides comprehensive RA management features. Lose It excels in general nutrition and weight management but lacks arthritis-specific functionality. For RA patients, Being Well offers the more targeted and comprehensive solution.
