Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about Being Well and RA management

What is Being Well?

Being Well is a personalized wellness app designed specifically for people with rheumatoid arthritis. It creates daily plans that adapt to your pain levels, energy, and goals, helping you manage your RA more effectively and improve your quality of life.

How does Being Well work?

The app learns your patterns and creates personalized daily plans that include gentle exercises, nutrition tips, stress management techniques, and medication reminders. It adapts to your energy levels and pain symptoms, ensuring you get the right support when you need it most.

Is Being Well free to use?

Yes! Being Well offers a free forever plan that includes basic features like personalized daily plans, symptom tracking, and educational content. Premium features are available for advanced analytics and additional support, but the core functionality is completely free.

Will Being Well replace my doctor?

No, Being Well is designed to complement your medical care, not replace it. The app helps you track symptoms, manage daily wellness, and make informed decisions about your health. Always consult with your healthcare provider for medical advice and treatment decisions.

How do I get started?

Getting started is easy! Simply download the app, create your profile, and answer a few questions about your RA symptoms and goals. The app will then create your first personalized wellness plan and guide you through your RA management journey.

Is my health data secure?

Absolutely. Your privacy and data security are our top priorities. All health data is encrypted and stored securely. You have complete control over your information and can delete your data at any time. We never share your personal health information with third parties.

Can I use Being Well with other RA apps?

Yes! Being Well is designed to work alongside other health apps and tools you might be using. You can export your data and share insights with your healthcare team. The app focuses on comprehensive wellness rather than just symptom tracking.

What if I have a flare-up?

Being Well includes special flare-up management features. When you report increased symptoms, the app automatically adjusts your daily plan to include more gentle activities, pain management techniques, and rest recommendations. It also helps you track flare patterns to identify triggers.
